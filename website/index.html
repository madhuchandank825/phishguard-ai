// How It Works
THREE STEPS TO SAFETY

PhishGuard AI analyzes URLs in real time using a trained machine learning model before you click.

01
🌐
You Visit a Page
The extension automatically detects the current URL when you navigate to any website.
02
🧠
AI Analyzes the URL
30+ features are extracted — TLD, keywords, HTTPS status, domain patterns, and more — and fed to the ML model.
03
🛡️
You Get a Verdict
Instant risk score and verdict: Safe, Caution, Warning, or Danger — with a detailed breakdown of suspicious signals.
04
🔍
Links Are Highlighted
Dangerous links on any webpage are automatically highlighted with warning icons so you know before you click.
